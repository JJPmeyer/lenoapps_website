# Pricing Plans

Data Bridge offers flexible pricing plans for teams of all sizes. Start on Free, then upgrade as your import volume and automation needs grow.

## Free Plan

$0/month

Perfect for trying Data Bridge and running small manual imports.

### What's Included

- All supported sources
- Import to standard boards (parent & subitems) and multi-level subitem boards
- Unlimited users
- Manual imports only
- Up to 3 imports per month

## Starter Plan

$14/month or $11/mo billed yearly ($132/year)

Best for teams running regular manual imports.

### What's Included

- All supported sources
- Import to standard boards (parent & subitems) and multi-level subitem boards
- Unlimited users
- Unlimited templates
- Up to 50 imports per month
- No saved schedules

## Pro Plan

$24/month or $19/mo billed yearly ($228/year)

Best for teams that need automation plus higher monthly capacity.

### What's Included

- All supported sources
- Import to standard boards (parent & subitems) and multi-level subitem boards
- Unlimited users
- Unlimited templates
- Up to 120 imports per month
- Up to 5 saved schedules

## Team Plan

$49/month or $39/mo billed yearly ($468/year)

Built for growing operations teams with frequent import runs.

### What's Included

- All supported sources
- Import to standard boards (parent & subitems) and multi-level subitem boards
- Unlimited users
- Unlimited templates
- Up to 250 imports per month
- Up to 10 saved schedules

## Business Plan

For high-volume teams that need scale and faster support.

## FAQ

### What counts as an import?

Each completed import run counts as 1 import, whether it is started manually or from a saved schedule.

### What happens if I exceed my monthly import limit?

New imports are blocked until your usage period resets, unless you upgrade your plan.

### Is there a free trial?

Yes. The Free plan is ongoing and includes up to 3 imports per month.

### Do you offer yearly pricing?

Yes. Starter, Pro, Team, and Business all include yearly billing options with lower effective monthly pricing.
